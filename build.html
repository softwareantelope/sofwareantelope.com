Setup

Subscribe

Your first no-risk step is to subscribe to our low cost CMS hosting service. You can cancel within the first 14 days, no questions asked.

You may already have a domain you wish to use, if so we will send you instructions to update your domain zone file to point at your website space or you may wish to transfer your domain to our professional registry.

Build

The next step is we will provide you with a no-frills content management website. This may include a Contact Us page, plus a basic framework of pages with menu items, with instructions on how to update it yourself. You can add as many pages as you require and link them in menus.

Publish

Publish your web presence from day one. Add content when you want, and every day you can publish news in a blog format for your customers. Add social networking and communicate with your target audience.

Design

Your content management site enables you to enter your own content using a basic template so you can build your content quickly.

We then offer a professional design solution that extends the functional template to brand your site and provide a fresh and engaging user experience.

We take a brief of your brand and functional requirements to produce a visual outline with an estimate of cost. With your design brief, we then can professionally implement it on your site. Please note, this is an additional cost to your basic subscription.

Agile Development

Agile development is a management process for rapid and progressive software implementation. It works for progressive web-development.

Stake-holders can track progress on their projects. Software Antelope uses a range of online tools that are a) simple to use, and b) allow us to collaborate with our clients, directly. Agile development methodology is supported by a task board (we use Trello.com) and a software repository (see our github.com link under Contact). This enables us to a) plan out each stage of delivery with full client oversight of actions agreed, and b) to deliver safe updates that provide constant progress. Each stage of development can be previewed and tested before release. Agile means constant reviewing of project goals, plus an ability to adapt to changing conditions and new ideas.

Waterfall Development

If you have prepared a specification we can provide an estimate on the scope of a project. Tracking a waterfall project is less flexible as goals should not change. Usually, we would expect a waterfall project to a) take longer to achieve any goals, and b) be more expensive, overall. It is sometimes preferable to implement a solid idea, but in our changing world, Agile development can adapt and is usually more productive.
