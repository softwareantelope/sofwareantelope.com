Hosting - resources available

Hosting a website need not be complicated. There are many options available but to offer you the best of both worlds, we support two basic forms of hosting, each has their own advantages. Click on the panels below for more information.

Shared Hosting

Shared Hosting provides many resources that are easily configured for you. Within a short period of time you can have a fully working CMS installation, customise a template that gives you immediate web presence. Email addresses and full site management are also available. We highly recommend WordPress for this purpose for its incredibly easy operation, rich set of template resources and plug in resources that support blogs, social media, ecommerce, galleries, publishing, and so much more.

Cloud Hosting

Once your basic website is operational and visible to the world with shared hosting, we can extend or move it to high availability, fast cloud based services. This is a better solution for busy sites and for specialised application development. If your site is high volume, we will quote the addition of required resources after monitoring site usage patterns.

Subscription

Software Antelope provdes a subscription service. Basic and Cloud. Your website is developed on our hosting resources and launched on our cloud. Your subscription fee includes all maintainance of your hosting resource including adding services. Services such as software development are charged for separately. When your resource development is complete, you can continue to host it with us by simply maintaining your subscription. Or you can move it to your own services, change your DNS settings and terminate your subscription. This provides a clear, simple, flexible and cost effective solution to both web development and hosting.

Basic £25 per month (cms starter sites, email)

Cloud £40 per month (busy sites, software development)

Bespoke Development Services

Agile Process

Discuss your website or software idea with our consultant and we will give you an estimate of how long it will take to develop and an approximate budget. We keep the risk factor low by defining broad development goals for each two week agile "sprint". Progress goals are posted on our interactive task management board. Each sprint we stage our development progress, and invoice you. It can then be "made live" when approprite to release your new project. Agile is best for progressive development typical of web projects.

Waterfall Process

You already have a specification, share it with our consultant and we may quote on it. Our terms for waterfall are generally 50% on commencement and 50% on delivery that may be spread over stages. Or you can engage our consultant on a daily rate basis.

Contract

Major contracts of engagement with Software Antelope are managed via Fuel Recruitment Agency. Feel free to get in touch with our developer directly in the first instance.

Consultancy

Onsite or remote services by a consultant developer with server and frontend skills to implement your business idea. We provide an estimate and invoice you every two weeks. For full profile of our consultant, see Linkedin profile.
