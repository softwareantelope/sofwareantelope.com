Your Domain

Your domain is your unique identity on the web. It consists of three parts: prefix.yourname.suffix.

.com is the most familar domain suffix. Location specific suffixes like .co.uk are also available.

A large range of meaningful suffixes are now available. For example: .london, .gallery, .technology, .company and hundreds of others to make a meaningful domain name for your business. The prefix can support multiple websites or services, for example: www. or mail. or cms.

You may have a domain or we can register one for you. We will provide zone file configuration details to attach your services.

Hosting a website need not be complicated. We support two basic forms of hosting, each having advantages. Click on the panels below for more information.

CMS Hosting

CMS (Content Management System) Hosting provides many resources configured for your website. Within a short period of time you can get your content on the web and then customise a template. We highly recommend WordPress for its straight forward operation, rich set of template resources and plug-in resources to support blogs, social media, ecommerce, galleries, publishing, and more.

Cloud Hosting

Once your basic website is operational and visible to the world with shared hosting, we can extend or move it to high availability, fast cloud based services. This is a better solution for busy sites and for specialised application development. If your site is high volume, we will quote the addition of required resources after monitoring site usage patterns.

Subscription

Software Antelope provides a subscription service. Basic and Cloud. Your website is developed on our hosting resources and launched on our cloud. Your subscription fee includes all maintainance of your hosting resource including adding services. Services such as software development are charged for separately. When your resource development is complete, you can continue to host it with us by simply maintaining your subscription. Or you can move it to your own services, change your DNS settings and terminate your subscription. This provides a clear, simple, flexible and cost effective solution to both web development and hosting.

    Agile Development

    A better way for stake-holders to track progress on their development projects. Software Antelope uses a range of online tools that are a) simple to use, and b) allow us to collaborate with our clients, directly. Agile development methodology is supported by a task board (we use Trello.com) and a software repository (see our github.com link under Contact). This enables us to a) plan out each stage of delivery with full client oversight of actions agreed, and b) to deliver secure updates that provide constant progress. Agile means constant reviewing of project goals, plus an ability to adapt to changing conditions and new ideas.

    Waterfall Development

    The "old fashioned" way to deliver software project is to plan every stage in a detailed specification document. This enables us to provide an estimate on the scope of a project. Tracking a waterfall project is less flexible as goals should not change. Usually, we would expect a waterfall project to a) take longer to achieve any goals, and b) be more expensive, overall. It is sometimes preferable to implement a solid idea, but in our changing world, Agile development is usually more appropriate.
