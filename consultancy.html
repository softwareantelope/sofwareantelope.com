Agile Development

A better way for stake-holders to track progress on their development projects. Software Antelope uses a range of online tools that are a) simple to use, and b) allow us to collaborate with our clients, directly. Agile development methodology is supported by a task board (we use Trello.com) and a software repository (see our github.com link under Contact). This enables us to a) plan out each stage of delivery with full client oversight of actions agreed, and b) to deliver secure updates that provide constant progress. Agile means constant reviewing of project goals, plus an ability to adapt to changing conditions and new ideas.

Waterfall Development

The "old fashioned" way to delivery software project is to plan everything up front, and expression of this in a detailed specification document. This enables us to provide an estimate on the scope of a project. Tracking a waterfall project is less flexible as goals should not change. Usually, we would expect a waterfall project to a) take longer to achieve any goals, and b) be more expensive, overall.
